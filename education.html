Learn About Waste Management

  • Collection: Gathering waste materials from homes, businesses, and other sources.
  • Transport: Moving collected waste to processing or disposal sites.
  • Processing: Treating waste materials to reduce volume, remove hazardous substances, and prepare for recycling or disposal.
  • Recycling: Converting waste materials into new products to prevent waste of potentially useful materials.
  • Disposal: Safely disposing of waste materials in landfills or through incineration.
